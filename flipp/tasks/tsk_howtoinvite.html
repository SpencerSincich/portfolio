How to invite a friend to collaborate on a Shared List

Inviting a friend to collaborate on a Shared List enables you to send an invitation to a friend asking them to collaborate on a shopping list.

Note: Your friend needs to have a Gmail or Facebook account to sign-up for the Flipp app.

To invite a friend to collaborate on a Shared List:

  1. Tap on the "Shopping List" button on the home page.

  2. Add the items and products that you want to place on the shopping list. See: How to add a product to your shopping list.
  3. Tap on the Invite button.

  4. Tap on the button: "Share Invitation Link".
  5. Tap on the name or logo of the program that you want to use to share the list.

    Your smartphone will then automatically load the program and display the page that you will use to message your friend.
  6. Type and send the message to your friend. The link to your shopping list will be attached to the message automatically.
  7. Wait for your friend to accept the invitation.

  8. Tap on the button: "OK, GOT IT".
  9. Start to collaborate on the list. Both you and your friend can add general items and specific products. On both apps, the changes will be updated in real-time.
