How to add a product to your shopping list

Adding a product to your shopping list enables you to create a shopping list that includes general items and specific products.

To add a product to your shopping list:

  1. Tap on the "List" button on the homepage.
  2. Tap on the "+" button.
  3. Tap on the "Add Item +" button.

    The keypad will pop-up.
  4. Type-in the name of the item that you want to add to the list. For example: "bread". The word will appear as you type.
  5. Tap on the auto-completed word.
  6. Tap on the right-pointing arrowhead that's on the right side of the screen.

    A new page will display all of the advertised products for the general item.
  7. Tap on the specific product that you want to view. For example: "Dempster's Whole Grain Bread" from the retailer "FreshCo".

    A new page will display the product on the retailer's flyer.
  8. Tap on the product to add it to your shopping list. A yellow circle will appear around the product.

    Also, the total number of items on your list will be updated on the "Shopping List" button at the bottom of the screen.
  9. Tap on the "Shopping List" button to view the page: "Shopping List Preview". You will see the product on the "My Clippings" list.
  10. To return to your shopping list, tap on the link: "GO TO FULL SHOPPING LIST".
