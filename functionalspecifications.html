Project description



This functional specifications project is a design document that shows the requirements for the web app of a fictional company called: VisoCast.


The app combines features of Netflix and Youtube.


Similar to Netflix, the app streams TV shows to users.


Similar to Youtube, the app enables users to post user generated videos (UGVs). The users can also interact with each other in a social media community.


The app is monetized by users paying-per-minute for the TV shows they watch. They can also opt to have the rate discounted if they watch commercials during the episodes.
