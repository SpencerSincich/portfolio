Project description



The Edvisor user guide is a freelance project.



I created the guide for the agents who work at the company: SSi Universal Education. SSi is an education management company that helps international students coordinate their visits to schools abroad.



    The Edvisor software is designed for customer relationship management (CRM). The SSi agents use the software to manage the processes for sending:
  • Quotes to students.
  • Applications to schools.
  • Invoices to students.
